Earth’s temperature has been rising by 0.14° Fahrenheit (0.08° Celsius) per decade since 1880, but the rate of warming since 1981 is more than twice that: 0.32° F (0.18° C) per decade.

2021 was the sixth-warmest year on record based on NOAA’s temperature data.

The reasons for this global warming is because of the harmful chemicals that are produced as a biproduct of human activities

A major contributor: Vehicles. Solution: Electric Vehicles

Cars produce many harmful emmisions such as - nitrogen dioxide, carbon monoxide, hydrocarbons, benzene, and formaldehyde.
They also produce Carbon dioxide which is the main culprit of global warming
As of 2022, there are about 1.446 billion cars in the world
An alternate to these vehicles run on "fossil fuels" are Electric Vehicles
They are environment friendly and there has been a lot of innovation in the field of EV, which mixed with great marketing and a generation that is gradually getting more and more environmentally cautious, there has been an great increase in the marketshare of electric vehicles

Just a beginning...

Although there has been a twofold growth in the sales of EVs, they still have a very small share in comparison to the total marketshare but the growing popularity, environmental awareness, marketing can lead to the growth getting further accelarated.

We can see the most EVs sold were vehicles and other types appear miniscule in terms of sales. This can be due to the LMV sector slowly and steadily becoming mainstream, especially with the advances of prominent names like Tesla

We can see that there is still a lot of innovation pending in this industy, by looking at the Battery operated (BEV) and Plug-In Hybrid (PHEV) powertrains, we can see how there is a demand for EVs. Other types of EVs such as Trucks, Vans and Buses are yet to evolve into the market.

Only developed countries have experienced the wave of EVs, but all the other countries are yet to feel the hit. As various countries start developing further and with advancement in EV production and technology, the affordability of an EV become better, we could expect the market to widen up and EV taking a big chunk of the market share away, as fossil fuels are depleting and becoming more and more expensive every day
